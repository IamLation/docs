### **openMenu**

Opens the main chop shop menu.

```javascript
exports.lation_chopshop:openMenu()
```

### **assignModel**

Starts a chop job (_must be called by the group owner_)

```javascript
TriggerServerEvent('lation_chopshop:assignModel')
```
