  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving the hotspot notification">
    Make sure your in-game phone resource is **started and active** before using **lation_diving**.

    Hotspot notifications are sent through the phone system. If the phone resource is not loaded before **lation_diving**, the notification will not be received.
  </Accordion>
